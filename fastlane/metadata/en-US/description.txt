It's the game that will be your "go to" for parties. Who's the Impostor is the fun and hilarious game that will keep you guessing and wondering which of your friends is being deceptive! From singing, to silly accents, to hotly contested facts -- use your best description to describe your own top secret word and convince your friends that you're not the Impostor! Play words from dozens of different themes from things you see around the house, celebrities, foods, pop references... or send in your own ideas! Features: - Play with two friends or up to 12 people. - Works in dozens of languages based on your phone settings. - When you're done each game you get a nice collage you can share. From all the deception with your friends, the fun will never stop! Game-nighters, your app is here.
